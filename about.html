    About

    Ryan Phillips is a clarinetist from Fairfax, Virginia currently studying at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill as a Kenan Music Scholar. He has been playing the clarinet since fifth grade and studied with Kenneth Lee throughout middle school and high school. Ryan has experience in wind ensembles, symphony orchestras, chamber groups, and theatrical pit orchestras.

    He has played in the following groups:

    • The Capitol Symphonic Youth Orchestra
    • Virginia Winds Academy’s Symphonic Winds
    • VBODA Senior Regional Orchestra
    • VBODA District XI All-District Symphonic Band
    • VBODA All-Virginia Concert Band.
    • The UNC Symphony Orchestra

    At UNC, Ryan is a member of Honors Carolina and is pursuing a double major in music and English. Outside of performing, Ryan enjoys reading, watching and writing about films, and listening to the music of Beethoven and Mahler. Ryan is a student of Donald Oehler.
